Johnathan Larroquette PMHNP

About Me

My name is Johnathan Larroquette, and I have been working in the mental health field for over 13 years. Throughout this time, I have worked with patients dealing with diverse mental health conditions. I have experience in acute psychiatric hospital settings, and I am passionate about helping others achieve better mental and physical health. I strive to problem-solve and help patients come up with personalized solutions to enhance their well-being.

My Approach to Therapy

My goal is to help patients find solutions for a more fulfilled experience in their daily lives. If deemed appropriate, I can provide medications to assist in treatment.

What to Expect in Our First Session

You can expect a positive, trusting, judgment-free, and calm environment where we will work together to find a solution tailored to your unique needs.
